Who are us ?

Founded in 2021, BFH'Optic was born from the passion of three devoted friends, Bakkari Walid, Fadiga Joachim and Haouali Soufil, hence the name BFH'Optic. With a clear vision of the future, we created a company dedicated to providing premium eyewear for everyone. Based in Paris, we pride ourselves on combining expertise, innovation and style to offer our customers an unrivaled online experience.

Our specificities

With the digital age, we seek to stand out and make the most of the capabilities of the Internet world. This is why we have chosen to use LiveStreaming during which our users will be able to participate and interact with our designers and experts. and animators.

Why choose us ?

- Premium quality: Our glasses are made with the highest quality materials, ensuring durability and comfort.
- Fast delivery: Receive your new glasses directly to your door in record time!
- Customer service: Our team is available to answer all your questions and chatbot system available 24/7!

Our mission

At BFH'Optic, we believe that every individual deserves to see the world with clarity and style. This is why we are committed to offering a varied range of frames and lenses, adapted to all needs and budgets. Our easy-to-use online platform allows you to choose, virtually try on and order your glasses with ease.
